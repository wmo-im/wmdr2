An observing facility, also referred to as station/platform (WMDS category 3) can be anything that supports making observations, e.g., a fixed station, moving equipment or a remote sensing platform. In abstract terms, an observing facility groups nearly co-located observing equipment managed by a single entity or several entities.<br/>The <b>gml:id</b> attribute shall be used to uniquely identify an observing facility globally by its WIGOS station identifier (WSI). Multiple WSIs for the same observing facility can be declared as a comma-separated list. By convention, the first entry is considered to be the primary WSI.<br/>The <b>gml:name</b> attribute shall be used to specify the (principal) name of the observing facility. <br/>The <b>gml:description</b> attribute can be used for non-formalized information about the location and surroundings, or the history of the observing facility (WMDS 4-05) at which an observation is made and that may influence it.<br/>
