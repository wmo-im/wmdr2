Deployed equipment or instrument used to make observations. <br/><b>gml:id</b> uniquely identifies the instrument. A WIGOS instrument identifier (WII) is recommended to be used. WIIs are of the form '1-a-b-c', where <br/>    a: Issuer of identifier<br/>    b: Issue of identifier<br/>    c: any combination of up to 16 letters and numbers<br/><br/><b>gml:name</b> may be used to provide a human readable name for an equipment or instrument.<br/><br/><b>gml:description</b> may be used to provide a human readable description of the equipment or instrument.<br/>
