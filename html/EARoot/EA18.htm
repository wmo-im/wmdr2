A WIGOS ObservationCollection is a grouping of homogeneous individual Observations covering a period of time. The time period covered is dictated by the earliest and latest validTime of the observations grouped. It may be reported explicitly using temporalCoverage.<br/><br/>ObservationCollections are bound to an Equipment ('Observer'). Thus, parallel deployments of multiple Equipments should not be grouped into a single ObservationCollection.<br/><br/>WIGOS ObservationCollection inherits from OMS Basic Observation ObservationCollection. Through this, it is also possible to group related ObservationCollections, e.g. to describe a product containing data of a various observed variables. <br/>
