A WMDR ObservingProcedure is a description of the steps involved in obtaining a result. Conceptually, every observation starts with a 'sampling', possibly involving some 'treatment' of the sample, followed by some analytical procedure that uses a 'method', followed by the application of some algorithm to generate the result. As part of an ObservationCollection, results may be further aggregated temporally or spatially before they are disseminated.  <br/><br/>The 'sampling' may take place 'in situ', 'remotely', or involve removal of a sample for 'ex situ' analysis.<br/><br/>
