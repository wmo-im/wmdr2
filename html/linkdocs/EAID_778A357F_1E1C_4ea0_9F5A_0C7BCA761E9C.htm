Changes from WMDR1.0

replaced
- GeospatialLocation becomes Location
- Equipment is split into AbstractEquipment ('catalogue entry') and Equipment ('instance')
- ObservingCapability becomes ObservationCollection with several more attributes, in part taken from the previous Deployment.

- The concept 'ObservationCollection.temporalCoverage' concept replaces the 'schedule' from WMDR1.0


removed
- AbstractEnvironmentalMonitoringFacility attributes are embedded explicity in ObservingFacility (Host) and Equipment (Observer)
- dataPolicy:DataPolicy is split into dataPolicy:DataPolicyType and attribution:Attribution
- deployment:Deployment
- schedule:Schedule is removed. Relevant information on the temporal coverage of an ObservationCollection can be expressed using ObservationCollection.temporalCoverage
- FeatureType MaintenanceReport removed; attribute maintenanceParty integrated into EquipmentLog
- FeatureType EventReport removed; attribute typeOfEevent integrated into FacilityLog
- Abstract class Log is removed
- MD_Distribution removed; Attribution covers important aspects

renamed
- ControlCheckReport becomes QualityAssuranceLog

new
- aggregation:Aggregation




moved to 'obsolete'
